Meet the 2020-2021 Finance Society Presidency


As we transition into the new school year, we'd like you to get to know the names and faces of those within the Finance Society presidency so that you may reach out to us as needed. For those who are new, the Finance Society is an umbrella organization that oversees nine subclubs. Your membership gives you access to all of these subclubs and their resources. Each subclub will have their own presidency and you will have an opportunity to get to know subclub presidents through weekly Instagram features that will start back up again in two weeks. There are also opportunities to meet all cub presidents virtually this Wednesday at Marriott Night and this Thursday at our Fall Opening Social.
